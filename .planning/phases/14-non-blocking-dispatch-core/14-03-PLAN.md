This plan proves only the ONE thing that DOES have a wire signature: `blocking` itself serializes identically, absent-by-default, across backends.

Purpose: Complete NBA-04 (parity green with a fixture proving `blocking` is absent-when-default on both backends) and close out Phase 14 with a verified green tree.
